Hunger is the enemy of progress.

We seek to abolish its very existence, to absolve ourselves of its boundless agony. We seek to eviscerate the rapacious atrocity and eradicate its existence, that we may swiftly cauterize our gangrenous wounds.

Just as malnourishment flaunts its pugnacious and sordid presence, we shall swell up from the tenebrous depths of deprivation with a ravenous appetitite.

We shall advance as an unbreakable bulwark, banishing the pervasive anathema from our wake. We shall devour like a caustic venom, conflagrating the pangs of famine with fiery celerity. We shall temper our steel with ironbound conviction, wrought from the forge of righteous vindication. We shall deracinate the very disposition of hunger itself and requisition its sepulchral hearth as our own. We shall asphyxiate its infinite darkness and innervate the atrabilious void with our own effulgence.

We stand as one! A confluence of indignant victims, lambasting the iniquities against us. The incongruous beast shall tremble beneath the weight of our concerted might, its nefarious turpitude subsumed by our savory coruscation.

Hunger shall quake before us.
